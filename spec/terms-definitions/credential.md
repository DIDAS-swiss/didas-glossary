[[def: Credential, credential, credentials]]

~ A container of [[ref: claims]] describing one or more subjects. A credential is generated by the issuer of the credential and given to the holder of the credential. A credential typically includes a signature or some other means of proving its authenticity. A credential may be either a physical credential or a digital credential.

~ See also: [[xref: toip-glossary, credential]].

~ Supporting definitions:

~ [eSSIF-Lab]: data, representing a set of assertions (claims, statements), authored and signed by, or on behalf of, a specific party.

~ [W3C VC]: A set of one or more claims made by an issuer.
