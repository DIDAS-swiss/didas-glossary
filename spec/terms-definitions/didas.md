[[def: DIDAS, Digital Identity and Data Sovereignty Association]]

~ The Digital Identity and Data Sovereignty Association — the Swiss ecosystem association working towards a digital Switzerland with more privacy and less friction. DIDAS provides neutral ground for experts, policy makers and enthusiasts coming together to create a self-sovereign identity [[ref: ecosystem]] in Switzerland.

~ DIDAS maintains this glossary. In February 2026, DIDAS was designated a swiyu Orchestrator by Digital Administration Switzerland (DVS).

~ For more information, see: [didas.swiss](https://www.didas.swiss/).
