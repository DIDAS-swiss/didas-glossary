[[def: ecosystem, ecosystems, Ökosystem, Ökosysteme, écosystème, ecosistema]]

~ A network of organizations that enables its participants to tap into the resources, capabilities, products and services of their partners. An ecosystem centers on a core product or service and is anchored around that core capability. Some organizations benefit from being at the center of their own ecosystem, while many benefit by being specialists or complementors in multiple other ecosystems.

~ Source: adapted from Mark Greeven, IMD.

~ In the DIDAS context, "ecosystem" usually refers to the swiyu trust ecosystem: the open, interoperable digital trust infrastructure established under the BGEID, in which public and private actors participate as issuers, holders and verifiers, interacting through common [[ref: protocol]]s under shared [[ref: governance]].
